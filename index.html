Welcome!

Jacob is a writer and instructional designer with over twenty years of experience in corporate training and writing instruction in Asia and North America. He specializes in accessibility and UX through learning management systems.

In addition to two degrees in writing and instructional design, he is currently enrolled in a graduate program in technical communication at the University of Minnesota. Using an XML editor, he is also researching, writing, and editing a book of letters his grandfather sent to his sister during his time on a submarine during WWII.

Jacob has over 20,000 hours of public speaking experience and has previously worked as a content writer and instructional designer with clients working in oil, nuclear energy, academia, and medicine.

He is able to manage multiple projects, consistently meet the required deadlines, and ensure all teams and clients have their needs met.
